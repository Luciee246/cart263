Game by Lucie Soussana, Jake Hayduk.

A game of making words with the given bigrams.

Instructions:

  • Up to 30 people can join the game
  • Choose a theme and difficulty level
  • Bigrams will appear on the screen
  • Players go back and forth answering their prompts
  • Players have 10 seconds to answer their own respective bigrams
  • Answer correctly, win coins!
  • Answer incorrectly, lose health!
  • Last person standing wins!

Rule Book:

  • Words must be at least 3 letters long
  • Words must include the bigram prompt
  • Gain 1 coin for every correct answer
  • Gain 3 extra coins for words long than 15 characters or longer
  • Gain 3 extra coins for hyphenated words
  • Gain 3 extra coins for maintaining a win streak of 5 or more
